Our Story

Fayriz Night began as a shared dream between three friends who wanted to create a space where people could escape the hustle of daily life. Inspired by French café culture and a love for feline companions, we've created a cozy sanctuary where you can enjoy quality coffee, delicious pastries, and the company of our resident cats.
